About Me

After a number of years in the financial services industry, I was ready for a new challenge. Upon much self-reflection and skill-set analysis, I decided to give coding a try and started messing around on CodeAcademy and Lynda.com. I was instantly hooked and decided to enroll at the software development bootcamp WeCanCodeIT to accelerate the learning and career change process. I will complete the program in December and look forward to expanding and incorporating my new-found knowledge to my knowledge-base and tackling real-world applications.

Born and raised in central Ohio, I was twice drawn back to the area after leaving, once for the Marines and again for graduate school. I currently live in Columbus with my wife Betheny, daughter Nora, and dog Chewbacca. In my spare time I enjoy spending time with friends and family, hiking, and tinkering in my woodshop.
